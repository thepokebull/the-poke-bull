How it works

Tauros turns its own trading fees into real graded Pokémon cards automatically opened live on Collector Crypt and airdropped to holders.

1

Creator rewards

Tauros is a Pumpfun token, every trade on it generates creator rewards that will fund Tauros.

2

Auto-claims

Once claimable rewards reach the active Gacha's threshold, Tauros claims them non-custodially.

3

Swap SOL → USDC

Claimed SOL automatically gets swapped to USDC, which is what Tauros uses to open packs.

4

Pokémon Packs

That USDC lets Tauros open Pokémon Gacha Packs on Collector Crypt. Each pull is a real, graded card tokenized as a programmable NFT.

5

Airdrops

Every pulled card is airdropped to an eligible $TAUROS holder. Wallets holding between 0.25% and 4% of the $TAUROS supply qualify, and larger holders are weighted higher.

6

Revealed live

The whole thing plays out on our site in real time: the machine opens, the slab flips, the card and its value are revealed, and it's airdropped to a holder.

Fair distribution

How airdrops work

Eligibility

Every card pulled by Tauros is airdropped to an eligible $TAUROS holder. To qualify, a wallet needs to hold between 0.25% and 4% of the supply. Wallets from 0.5% to 4% get full weight, and wallets from 0.25% to 0.5% are included at half weight, so larger holders are picked about twice as often.

Under the hood

The tech

PF

Pumpfun

Tauros and its creator-reward stream. The fuel for the entire loop.

JUP

Jupiter

On-chain SOL→USDC swaps at the best available route, keeping only a gas reserve in SOL.

CC

Collector Crypt

Tauros and the cards. Real graded Pokémon cards tokenized as programmable NFTs, redeemable for the physical card.

RPC

Helius (Solana)

Reads holder distribution, wallet balances, and card metadata via the Solana DAS API.

SSE

Live feed

Server-sent events push every pull and status change to all viewers instantly, keeping the reveals synchronized.

SEC

Guardrails

Per-cycle spend caps, a gas reserve, and a re-entrancy lock, so fast-arriving fees never overlap or overspend.

Trust

Transparency

  • ✓Everything is on-chain. Claims, swaps, pack opens, and airdrops are all real Solana transactions you can verify.
  • ✓Every pull is public. The reveal is shown live, and every card is logged in “Latest Pulls” and on the Airdrops page, each with a link to its airdrop transaction.
  • ✓Cards go to holders. Nothing is sold off. Each pulled card is airdropped, fairly, to the community.
  • ✓Real assets. Collector Crypt cards are graded, vaulted, and redeemable for the physical card.
